SKATE TECHNIQUES

Old School


Old school skating came to fruition during the 1960s when surfers decided they wanted to provide the same feelings of being on waves onto the urban world and streets. As a result mini surfboards were first released which allowed for trucks and wheels for a consistent roll. Over the next ten years skating was adapted to suit the thrill seekers looking for the same buzz as surfing. This is where the original old school tricks really shined through. Issue was most people missed the old surfing ways of the past and strived for more thrills and risks. Until a few bored skaters decided to dive bomb into an empty pool.
