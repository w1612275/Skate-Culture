HUF

Huf was created by a man named Keith Hufnagel. Keith grew up as a huge stake culture fanatic spending a lot of time dedicated to learning and understanding it. This resulted in a deep passion that led him to starting to limited huf brand we see today. The brand uses simplistic designs of its attractive logos and sold in drops, like you'd expect from top designer brands like off white, supreme, palace etc. These days it's easy enough to get a nice piece from their store or other sites but their collabs are almost unseen with many ranging in the couple hundreds.
