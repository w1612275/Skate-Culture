Njyah Huston

Nyjah Huston born in davis, california is a new era skater riding at his very best as of late. He is boasting an incredible 12 sponsorships including big names like mountain dew, doritos, element and many more, making him one of the skating communities richest men and still got years left due to his young age of 22 allowing him an easy 10 more years of amazing next level skating.. Nyjah skates a very modern technique taking park in SKATE tournaments of elimination and destroying the scene wherever there's a box, grind rail or small ramp. But not everything was easy for him. When he was young it is known that nyjah had a very strict rastafarian upbringing up till his adult years with parents who forced him into reggae music only and was instructed never to cut his dreads, unfortunately for his parents though, they didn't stay for very long when he turned pro in 2010.

Tony Hawk

With over 60 1st place victories in numerous levels of skate competition, Tony Hawk is the most successful skater of all time. Also know for the name birdman due to his tremendous skills, landing some of skating's most difficult and unique ramp tricks of all time. Tony has proven himself to be an amazing skater and businessman. The trick that bought him the most fame was his 900 air which requires the skater the achieve 2 and a half full rotations in mid air. This also helped build the opportunity for the father of 4 to release a series of huge skating games adored by people even today. As well as this, he was able to create the skate brand Birdhouse that still sells to this day.
