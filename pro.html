Tony Hawk

With over 60 1st place victories in numerous levels of skate competition, Tony Hawk is the most successful skater of all time. Also know for the name birdman due to his tremendous skills, landing some of skating's most difficult and unique ramp tricks of all time. Tony has proven himself to be an amazing skater and businessman. The trick that bought him the most fame was his 900 air which requires the skater the achieve 2 and a half full rotations in mid air. This also helped build the opportunity for the father of 4 to release a series of huge skating games adored by people even today. As well as this, he was able to create the skate brand Birdhouse that still sells to this day.
