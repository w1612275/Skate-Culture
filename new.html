SKATE TECHNIQUES

New School

The most recent era of skating which is commonly known as new school skating, reverts back to the original heritage of riding, but with a few important modifications that lead to its huge popularity. Essentially this era relies entirely on the foot and legs to put off tricks. The hand is therefore to allowed to come into contact when attempting this. As well as this, more urban areas are using to perform the more skilled tricks which is usually found at grind rails and small stair cases that allow for lots of air without having to rely on the ramps to propel the skater.
